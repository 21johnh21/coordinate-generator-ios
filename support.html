Frequently Asked Questions

How do I generate random coordinates?

1. Open the sidebar (tap the menu button on mobile)
2. Select a region from the dropdown (World, Continent, Country, or Near Me)
3. Tap the "Generate Location" button
4. Your random coordinates will appear on the map!

What is the "Near Me" feature?

The "Near Me" feature generates random coordinates within a specific radius of your current location. When you first use this feature, you'll be asked to grant location permission. Your location is used only for this calculation and is never stored or shared.

How do I change the map style?

Tap the layers button (square icon with layers) in the top-right corner of the map to switch between dark mode and satellite view.

Can I copy coordinates?

Yes! After generating coordinates, tap the "Copy" button to copy them to your clipboard. You can also tap "Open in Maps" to view the location in Apple Maps.

Where is my coordinate history stored?

Your coordinate history is stored locally on your device only. It's never uploaded to any server. You can view your history in the sidebar and clear it at any time by tapping "Clear History".

Does the app work offline?

The app requires an internet connection to load map tiles from Mapbox. However, once loaded, basic coordinate generation works offline.

Why can't I see the map?

Make sure you have a stable internet connection. The app uses Mapbox to display maps, which requires connectivity. If you continue to have issues, try restarting the app.

How do I manage location permissions?

On iOS:
1. Open Settings
2. Scroll to Geo Roulette
3. Tap Location
4. Choose your preferred permission level

Is my data private?

Yes! Geo Roulette does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All processing happens locally on your device. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Are there any ads or in-app purchases?

No! Geo Roulette is completely free with no ads and no in-app purchases. Enjoy the full app experience at no cost.

Troubleshooting

App won't load

  • Check your internet connection
  • Force close and restart the app
  • Make sure you're running the latest iOS version
  • Try restarting your device

Map is blank or not loading

  • Ensure you have a stable internet connection
  • Try switching between map styles (dark/satellite)
  • Check if other apps can access the internet
